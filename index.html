Rates

A study of rates of postale items during Ahmad Shah era (1909 - 1925)

Letters

In this period, the rate of a letter up to 2 Miskal was 6 Chahi. For each extra Miskal the rate would go up by 3 Chahi.

6 Chahi

A cover sent from Ram Hormoz on 21/4/14 to Bushire, received on 4/5/14. The cover was routed through Bandar Naseri on 23/4/14 and then Mohammareh on 29/4/14. It is tied to the 6ch Ahmad Shah portrait issue of 1911.

9 Chahi

A cover sent from Bouchir to Kerman, recived on 1/6/1915. The cover went thrugh Shiraz on 13/5/15 and Yezd on 22/5/15. It is tied by 9ch Coat of Arms issue of 1909 with 1333 Hedgira overprint (the dotted variety).

12 Chahi

A cover from Kerman sent on 3/4/1911 to Yezd, tied by a 10ch Coat of Arms issue of 1909 and 2 registeration labels used as 1ch.
