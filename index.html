Iran Postal History: 1909-1925

Welcome to my website on Iranian postal history. My name is Masoud Valafar and I specialize in the postal history of Iran from 1909 to 1925, during the reign of Ahmad Shah. This period, when Iran was known as Persia, was marked by significant turbulence, which is reflected in the postal services of the time.

Website Structure:

Our site is organized into four main sections:

  • Education: Discover general information about letters and postal markings from this era.
  • Rate: Access detailed information about postal item rates during this period.
  • Collection: Explore a curated exhibition of notable items from our personal collection.
  • Resources: Find a list of relevant research materials and references.

Ongoing Research

This website represents an ongoing research project aimed at becoming a comprehensive source on the postal history of early 20th century Iran. As with any historical research, this is a continually evolving endeavor. We welcome your insights and contributions to help enhance the accuracy and completeness of our information.
