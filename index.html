Welcome to my website dedicated to Iran Postal History! My name is Masoud Valafar and my main focus is on the Iran postal history from 1909 to 1925, when Ahmad Shah was in power. Iran, known as Persia at the time, was experiencing one of its most chaotic time in its history and this chaos is in turn reflected in the postal service at the time.

This website is organized as follows:

  • On the Education page, you can find general information about the letters and postal markings of that era.
  • On the Rate page, I have listed all the information about the rate of postal items.
  • On the Collection page I am exhibiting some of the interesting items I have in my collection.
  • On the Resources page, I have listed all the relevant resources I have found.

This website is an ongoing research project to become an exhustive source for the postal history of that era. It is by no means anywhere close to being complete or will ever be. Please check my work and contact me at mvalafar84@gmail.com if you have any comments or suggestions!

Finally and most importantly, I want to thank everyone who has help me enjoy the interesting and complicated details of Iran postal history.
